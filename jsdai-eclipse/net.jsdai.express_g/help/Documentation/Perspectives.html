EXPRESS-G Perspective

The EXPRESS-G perspective presents needful viewers and editors to make the work with Graphical EXPRESS Editor more convenient  (see Figure 1):
  • Navigator panel- the list of files, presented in the selected workspace.
  • Console panel- the application log details.
  • Express-G schema Editor panel - the graphical layout of the selected schema.
  • Outline panel - the list of schemas with schema diagrams situated beneath.
  • Express-G Outline panel - the hierarchy of EXPRESS objects of the selected schema layout in the Outline view.
