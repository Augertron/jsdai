Justify

You can justify selected objects in the following ways:
  • Left / Center / Right
  • Top / Center / Bottom

Left / Center / Right

You can arrange the selected objects according to the top object's left/center/right on the diagram.
  • Select objects to be justified on the diagram (see Figure 1).

Figure 1. Selected objects on the diagram
  • Right click on the selection area. The context sensitive menu appears.
  • Select the Justify --> Left menu item.
  • The objects are arranged according to the top object left side (see Figure 2). Also you can justify according the center or the right side of the top object by selecting the particular context menu item.

Figure 2. Objects are arranged according to the left side of the top object

Top / Center / Bottom

You can arrange the selected objects according to the object, situated on the left of the diagram.
  • Select objects to be justified on the diagram (see Figure 1).
  • Right click on the selection area. The context sensitive menu appears.
  • Select the Justify --> Top/Center/Bottom menu item.
  • The objects are arranged according to the object, situated on the left (see Figure 3).

Figure 3. Objects arranged according to the bottom of the left object.
