Relationship Hints

The user has a possibility to change the appearance of the relationship line and name:
  • Move relationship line and/or connection place
  • Move relationship name
  • Reset placement hints
After you select an applicable relationship, the relationship is highlighted on a diagram (see Figure 1).


Figure 1. Relationship with hints

Move Relationship Line and/or Connection place

To change the relationship line and/or connection place with an object:
  • Point on the highlighted square on the relationship line.
  • Drag with mouse to an applicable direction and drop.

Move Relationship Name

To change a location of the relationship name:
  • Point on the highlighted circle on the relationship line.
  • Drag with mouse to an applicable direction and drop.

Reset Placement hints

After the relation line was moved to the applicable direction, you can restore an initial hints place of the selected relation:
  • Select the relationship. The relationship line is highlighted (see Figure 2).
         
                Figure 2. Highlighted relation line
  • Right click on the selected relationship line. The context sensitive menu appears.
  • Select the Reset placement hints menu item. The initial place of the relation hints will be set (see Figure 3).
         
                Figure 3. Initial relation hints placement is set.
