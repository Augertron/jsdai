Schema Level Diagram

A schema level diagram consist of  the representation of single and multiple schemas and their interfaces.
The contents of an EXPRESS-G schema level diagram are limited to the schemas comprising  the diagram and the schema interface (see Figure 1). The following are included:
  • scehmas that refer to another schema by means of USE;
  • schemas that refer to another schema by means of REFERENCE;
  • names of the things that are referenced or used.
