Relation Routing

The relation can be routed from one box to another in three different shapes (see Figure 1):
  • "C" shape for outer connection of the box top to top, left to left, right to right and bottom to bottom.
  • "L" shape connects top/bottom to left/right sides of the box.
  • "S" shape connects left to right and top to bottom.
The relation routing shape is selected by the hint point place. There is the area for every shape. When the hint point is in the specific area, the relation is always has  the particular shape.
